Do NOT dismiss or deprioritize PR review comments just because they come from a bot (Copilot, Claude, etc.). These bots are configured on the repo for a reason — their feedback is legitimate and should be evaluated on merit, not authorship.

**Why:** User was frustrated when I suggested dismissing Copilot comments as if they were less important than human comments. The bots are there to catch real issues.

**How to apply:** When reading PR review comments, evaluate each comment on its technical merit regardless of whether it came from a human or a bot. Never suggest dismissing comments based on who posted them.
